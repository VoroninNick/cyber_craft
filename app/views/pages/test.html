Full startup support and integration

We will help you integrate new employees into your existing team and will provide full startup support to work out the most efficient cooperation model for your distributed team, to establish communication and reporting procedures. You will have full control over product development and will communicate with and manage your remote developers directly. And we will take care of everything else pertaining to hiring and housing them at our office in Ukraine:

  • Facilities and technical infrastructure— workstation, office space, system administrators, procurement and licensing;

  • Communication and HR management— communication planning, full scope of recruitment and replacement activities;

  • All administrative, legal and financial activities— daily full-cycle operational support;

  • Performance monitoring and improvement— constant professional development of your team, knowledge-gaining and sharing environment;

  • Retention activities— participation in different conferences, trainings, team building and corporate activities, sport events.
